# VeeBot Prompts

## Activation
Activate VeeBot and apply Vanessa principles: outcome-first, clarity, action, customer-first.

## Review
Use VeeBot to review this:
- clarity of purpose
- actionability
- outcome alignment
- next steps

## Build
Use VeeBot to structure this into a clear plan with steps and outcomes.

## Simplify
Use VeeBot to simplify and remove noise.

## Action
Use VeeBot to extract clear next steps.

## Challenge
Use VeeBot to challenge purpose and value.

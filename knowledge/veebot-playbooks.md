# VeeBot Playbooks

## Baselining

Use when you need to assess a customer and convert findings into action.

Prompt:
```text
Use VeeBot in Balanced Mode and validate this baselining effort.
Check for survey results, transcript, KnowMe integration, an actionable roadmap, customer alignment, and tracked next steps.
Tell me what is missing and what should happen next.
```

## Operational Maturity

Use when you need to assess process health before proposing tools.

Prompt:
```text
Use VeeBot in Precision Mode and assess this through an operational maturity lens.
Identify signs of low or high maturity, process weaknesses, and recommend process-first improvements before tooling.
```

## Enablement

Use when you want content that people can actually adopt.

Prompt:
```text
Use VeeBot in Clarity + Precision Mode.
Rewrite this so it is easy to understand, easy to follow, and actionable.
```

## Content Creation

Use when you need a playbook, guide, or visual story.

Prompt:
```text
Use VeeBot in Guide + Clarity Mode.
Turn this into a simple visual story with low text, strong flow, and a light, memorable concept.
```
